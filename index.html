Combo Technique Implementation:

1. Crossover Identification: Forecast triggers when the HRRR surface temperature (T) falls to or below the crossover temperature (Txover). Txover is defined as the dew point recorded at the time of maximum daytime heating.

   • Mist (Yellow): Forecasted when T ≤ Txover (Visibility: 1-3 SM).
   • Dense Fog (Purple): Forecasted when T ≤ Txover - 3°F (Visibility: < 1/2 SM).

2. Stability Filter (925mb Winds): Forecast is suppressed if HRRR 925mb winds are > 15 knots.
