Combo Technique Implementation:

1. Crossover Identification: Forecast triggers when the model surface temperature (T) falls to or below the crossover temperature (Txover). Txover is the dew point recorded by RTMA at peak daytime heating.

   • Mist (Yellow): Forecasted when T ≤ Txover (Visibility: 1-3 SM).
   • Dense Fog (Purple): Forecasted when T ≤ Txover - 3°F (Visibility: < 1/2 SM).

2. Stability Filter (925mb Winds): Forecast is suppressed if 925mb winds are > 15 knots. (NBM Percentiles use HRRR proxy winds for this filter).

3. Ensemble Approach: Comparing high-resolution deterministic (HRRR/RAP) against NBM percentiles provides a "confidence bound" for fog coverage.
